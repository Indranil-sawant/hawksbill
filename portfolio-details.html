Project Overview

Our client, a major pharmaceutical research facility, required a reliable source of high-purity nitrogen for their sensitive LC-MS applications. Relying on cylinders was proving to be costly and logistically challenging.

Hawksbill Technik proposed and implemented a centralized Nitrogen Generation System specifically designed to meet the flow and purity requirements of multiple LC-MS instruments. The system eliminates the need for cylinders, providing on-demand nitrogen with consistent purity.

The installation included the generator unit, a buffer tank, and an integrated oil-free compressor, all enclosed in a sound-proof cabinet to maintain a quiet laboratory environment.

The Challenge

Ensuring uninterrupted supply of 99.999% purity nitrogen for 24/7 LC-MS operation without the safety risks and downtime associated with high-pressure cylinders.

Our Solution

A redundant PSA Nitrogen Generator system with automatic changeover and remote monitoring capabilities, ensuring zero downtime and consistent gas quality.
