FITOK Instrumentation

High-performance Valves, Pressure Regulators, and Fittings for industrial safety and precision.

Authorized Distributor
Value Engineered Solutions

Precision Engineering for Critical Applications

Hawksbill Technik serves as an authorized distributor and technical support partner for FITOK, a global leader in high-quality instrumentation. We provide comprehensive fluid system solutions designed to withstand extreme pressures and temperatures, ensuring safety and efficiency in your operations.

Our portfolio includes a vast range of valves, fittings, and regulators manufactured to ISO 9001:2015 standards. Every component is rigorously tested—valves are nitrogen tested at 6,000 psig to guarantee leak-free performance. Whether for Pharmaceutical R&D, Oil & Gas pipelines, or Petrochemical processing, we deliver reliability you can trust.

Technical Specifications

High Pressure Rating

Components tested and rated up to 6,000 psig (41.4 MPa) for extreme environments.

Zero Leakage

100% Nitrogen tested to ensure absolute seal integrity and safety for hazardous gases.

Premium Material

Available in SS 316, Hastelloy, Monel, and Brass for superior corrosion resistance.

Certified Quality

ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing with CE marking for global compliance.
